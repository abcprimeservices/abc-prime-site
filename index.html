The Problem

You're already generating leads. You're just losing too many of them.

Contractors and home service operators are in the field, on a ladder, under a sink, or managing a crew. Nobody's sitting at a desk waiting to answer calls. The lead calls, nobody picks up, and they move on to the next name on Google. That's not a marketing problem — that's a response problem. And it's completely fixable.

What's actually happening right now

A homeowner needs an HVAC tune-up. They call three numbers. The first one that calls back gets the job. If that's not you — it's your competitor.

What should be happening

The moment a call is missed, the lead gets a text. They reply. They book. You show up. The system handled the rest while you finished the job you were already on.

The real cost

One missed call in roofing is worth $8,000–$25,000. In HVAC it's $3,000–$12,000. Most businesses miss 30–50% of their inbound calls. The math is brutal.
